Ethical & Trustworthiness Concerns

Concerns about AI accuracy ("hallucinations") and trustworthiness are growing, impacting sectors like the judiciary. Developers' trust in AI outputs is also worsening.

  • Judicial guidelines for generative AI due to "hallucinations."
  • Developers' trust in AI outputs worsening (Stack Overflow).
  • Concerns over "synthetic media" in federal policy.

Cybersecurity & AI Arms Race

AI is being used for both advanced cyberattacks and defense, creating a dynamic arms race. New firms are emerging to tackle AI security at the source.

  • Palo Alto Networks acquires CyberArk ($25B) for identity security.
  • New firms like Dawnguard and Cyata focusing on AI security.
  • AI's dual-use nature in both offense and defense.
